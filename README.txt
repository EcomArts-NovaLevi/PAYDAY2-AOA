Argent Ordinance Armory - Weapon Overhaul

(Fmrly: Dr. Peterson's Full Telekill Arsenal)  This mod for PAYDAY 2 is an overhaul to most all of the weapons in PAYDAY 2 as of the date of the latest update!

Balances, tweaks, buffs, and nerfs various features of most (if not all) primary and secondary weapons in the game.
