Dr. Peterson's Full Telekill Arsenal - Weapon Overhaul

This mod for PAYDAY 2 is an overhaul to most all of the weapons in PAYDAY 2 as of the date of the latest update!

The aim of this mod is not balance, but rather just to mod the weapons in a way that myself and my friends find fun.
I am well aware that it is destructive and unbalanced.  The aim is not to make it balanced, though I may create a version of this that does attempt such.
